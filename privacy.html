Privacy Policy

This is a personal-use application. It does not collect, store, share, or sell personal information of any user.

The application accesses only the TikTok account of its single owner, using OAuth tokens stored locally on the owner's device. Tokens are never transmitted to any third party.

The application uses the official TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content prepared by the owner.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.
